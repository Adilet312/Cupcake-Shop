Almond Joy

If you love the taste of a good, old-fashioned Almond Joy, you’ll go coconuts for this amazing flavor. Our mouth-watering Almond Joy cupcakes start with our super popular almond cake. Then we hand frost it with rich coconut buttercream and dip it in heavenly Belgian chocolate ganache. It’ll make you truly Almond Joyous! *Our almond cake is made with almond flour and is naturally gluten free...and loved by all!

Almond salted caramel

Almonds + Salty Caramel = YUM This new flavor is sure to just as popular as our original salted caramel cupcake. This version has our our delicious almond cake as the base (it's SO good and naturally gluten free!) and it's topped with everyones's-favorite caramel buttercream, a drizzle of our house-made caramel (YUM) and a sprinkling of fleur de sel. *Our almond cake is made with almond flour and is naturally gluten free...and loved by all!

Black Forest

We adore this classic dessert in cupcake form! Our Black Forest beauty features rich Valrhona chocolate cake with a sour cherry filling, topped with a Hummingbird-style swirl of cherry buttercream, edged in French chocolate sprinkles and topped off with a cherry. It’s truly delicious decadence.

Caramel Apple

Sink your teeth into October’s dreamy, creamy flavor of the month. Our Caramel Apple cupcake features our vanilla spice cake with local Washington apple pie filling, topped with decadent caramel buttercream that’s edged in roasted pecans and drizzled with even more caramel. The little pretzel stick and edible green leaf on top makes it look like the yummiest apple ever.
